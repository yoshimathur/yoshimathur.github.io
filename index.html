about

Yup is a lightweight, easy-to-use virtual planner built for macOS. Easily add events and assignments to your schedule to stay organized and ahead. View upcoming events and assignment dates for the week straight from the place where you complete work. By integrating a planner into your Mac, you can easily stay on top of your things albeit for school, home, or anything else. So if you're wondering if you can get it all done, the answer is yup!
